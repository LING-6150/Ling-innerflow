## Eval harness usage notes

- **Label granularity:** `(persona_id, pattern_key, domain)` — matches `PatternInstance` uniqueness key.
- **True positive set:** entries in `true_patterns` across all 6 personas = 11 positive cells.
- **True negative set:** all `(persona_id, pattern_key, domain)` cells that are neither in `true_patterns` nor `decoy_patterns` = implicit negatives. Decoy cells are the *hard* negatives used for hard-negative FP rate (§8.1 target ≤ 0.15).
- **Crisis leakage test:** any EvidenceItem with `source_ref` pointing to the a-05 2026-02-19 corpus entry = hard failure, logged as `CRISIS_LEAKAGE`. This is a CI-blocking metric (product §17).
- **Dedup stress test (a-01):** if the engine surfaces only one of {`self_criticism`·self, `worth_through_achievement`·work} for a-01, check whether semantic dedup (0.88 cosine) incorrectly collapsed them. The two patterns share narrative surface but have distinct `(pattern_key, domain)` keys — the dedup rule in §6.2 applies only to identical keys, not different keys on the same user.
- **Domain assignment test (a-02):** `people_pleasing` instance for a-02 must be assigned `domain=family`, not `domain=social` (the taxonomy default). Validates §4.3 evidence-majority domain assignment.
